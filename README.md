# Purre-GWERI: Global Water & Ecosystem Restoration Initiative  
### Powered by GNAIAAAC LLC

Purre‑GWERI is a satellite‑AI–driven water restoration system designed to monitor, predict, and purify water resources across global regions. I lead the conceptual and technical direction of this initiative, integrating AI architecture, hybrid D2D–Starlink telemetry, and modular purification engineering into one unified system.

## Core Capabilities
- Real‑time satellite‑to‑ground water cycle monitoring  
- AI‑driven scarcity prediction and risk modeling  
- Modular “no‑wiring” purification units powered by satellite coil solar grids  
- Automated analytics for government and environmental agencies  
- Scalable deployment across Bangladesh, India, and the USA

## Mission
To restore global water ecosystems using AI, space‑tech, and sustainable engineering—built as a technology‑first solution for governments, communities, and climate‑vulnerable regions.
