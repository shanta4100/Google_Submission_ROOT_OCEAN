# Executive Summary

Our project leverages Recursive AI to autonomously create AI agents that monitor, optimize, and expand access to clean water worldwide. The ROOT_OCEAN_PURE_WATER module collects real-time ocean water data, purifies it autonomously, and feeds environmental metrics back to AI agents for continuous optimization.

We aim to empower communities, accelerate sustainable water innovation, and enhance global wellbeing by combining advanced AI systems with hands-on educational programs. Pilot programs are designed to demonstrate measurable improvements in water quality, workforce development, and scalable environmental impact.
