# ROOT_OCEAN_PURE_WATER – Pitch Deck Outline

## 1. Title & Mission
- Mission: AI-driven, satellite-informed water purification and monitoring for governments and communities.

## 2. Problem
- Unsafe water, aging infrastructure, and limited real-time visibility.

## 3. Solution
- AI engine + satellite telemetry + sensors.
- Modular purification kits and dashboards.

## 4. Core Technology
- AI models, satellite data, D2D/Starlink connectivity, cloud dashboards.

## 5. Use Cases
- Municipal water safety, contamination response, rural monitoring.

## 6. Impact
- Faster detection, reduced incidents, scalable adoption.

## 7. Implementation Plan
- Prototype → Pilot → Regional → National scaling.

## 8. Partnerships
- Municipal, regional, tribal water authorities.

## 9. Sustainability
- Open-source components, governance, long-term maintenance.

## 10. Request
- Funding, technical support, and pathways to multi-government deployment.
