# Technical Overview

## Recursive AI Workflow
The system is built on a Recursive AI architecture where a Parent AI generates and manages Child AI agents. These agents are deployed into real-world environments, continuously learning and optimizing through feedback loops.

Flow:
Parent AI → Child AI → ROOT_OCEAN_PURE_WATER Modules → Feedback → Optimization

---

## ROOT_OCEAN_PURE_WATER Module
This module focuses on water intelligence and sustainability:
- Real-time ocean water monitoring
- Autonomous purification processes
- Water recycling and reuse systems
- Community-level feedback integration

---

## Technology Stack
- Gemini 3 Ultra (AI processing)
- LangChain (AI orchestration)
- AutoGPT (autonomous agents)
- Pinecone (vector database)
- H100 / H200 GPUs (compute infrastructure)

---

## Outputs
- Real-time environmental dashboards
- Water quality monitoring reports
- AI performance and learning metrics
- Predictive optimization insights
