Overview

The Data Fabric is the foundational intelligence layer of the Global AI Black Box. It integrates environmental, health, economic, and coordination datasets into a unified, sovereign, and ethically governed data ecosystem. This fabric powers all analytics, modeling, and decision intelligence across the system.

Core Data Domains

  • Environmental Data — climate indicators, hydrological data, ecological metrics, restoration datasets.
  • Public Health Data — epidemiological records, wellbeing metrics, resilience indicators.
  • Coordination Data — institutional signals, multi‑agent states, cross‑sector alignment metrics.
  • Planetary Metrics — unified indicators for global stability, risk, and restoration progress.
  • Infrastructure Data — energy, water, logistics, and critical systems intelligence.

Data Governance

All data within the Global AI Black Box follows strict sovereignty, transparency, and ethical alignment principles. The system ensures:

  • Secure access and encrypted storage
  • Auditability and traceability
  • Responsible use and ethical safeguards
  • Institutional and cross‑sector compliance
